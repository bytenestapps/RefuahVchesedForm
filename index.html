Gemach Medical Equipment

Volunteers in our medical equipment gemach help ensure that essential items reach those who need them most. Responsibilities include delivering equipment such as oxygen concentrators, IV poles, wheelchairs, walkers, and other medical supplies to those who need them, and pick them up when they are no longer needed and returning them to our inventory.
